Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Nirvan AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Data Collection & Privacy-First Policy

Local-First Storage: At Nirvan AI, we believe your personal growth is private. Most data, including your chat history and personal context, is stored locally on your device only.

  • Chat History: Stored locally via AsyncStorage. We do not store your conversations on our servers.
  • Personal Context: Any values or goals you set are stored on-device.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide our AI coaching services, we utilize the following trusted partners:

  • OpenAI: Your messages are sent to OpenAI (GPT-4) to generate coaching responses. This data is subject to OpenAI's privacy policies and is not used to train their models for other users.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and ensure secure payment processing via the Apple App Store. No personal chat data is shared with RevenueCat.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption for all API communication and local data isolation.
